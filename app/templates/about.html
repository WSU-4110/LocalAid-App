Our Mission

Connecting neighbors through acts of kindness and goodwill.

LocalAid was founded in 2023 with a simple idea: what if we could make it easier for people to help each other in their communities without the complications of money or transactions?

We believe that everyone has something to offer, and everyone sometimes needs a little help. By facilitating these connections, we strengthen communities one kind act at a time.
