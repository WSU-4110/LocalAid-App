LocalAid connects people who need help with neighbors willing to provide assistance. You can post requests for help (like errands, tutoring, or companionship) or browse requests in your area to offer help. All help is volunteer-based - no money changes hands.

Yes! LocalAid is completely free to use. We believe in keeping the platform accessible to everyone in the community. There are no fees for posting requests or offering help.

  • Always meet in public places when possible
  • Tell a friend or family member about your plans
  • Check user ratings and reviews before meeting
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, don't proceed
  • For home visits, consider having someone else present

Absolutely! When posting a request, you can specify any requirements. For example, if you need tutoring help, you might request someone with teaching experience. If you need help with home repairs, you might ask for someone with carpentry skills. However, remember this is volunteer help, not professional services.

After a help request is completed, both parties can leave a rating (1-5 stars) and optional review. These help build trust in the community. Ratings are visible on user profiles. We encourage honest feedback while being respectful.

You can edit or delete your requests/offers at any time from your dashboard. If you've already connected with someone, please be courteous and message them to let them know about the cancellation.
